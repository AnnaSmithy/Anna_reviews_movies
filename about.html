About the site

Mara Vale

Mara Vale is the fictional sole editor and critic behind The Late Screening, a small, rigorously maintained review site she updates after weekday press screenings and late weekend repertory runs.

Raised in Bristol and now based in Manchester, Mara started the site after years of writing capsule notes in the margins of festival brochures and train tickets. Her taste runs toward patient direction, exact performances, and films that know the difference between seriousness and self-importance. She is skeptical of bloated prestige cinema, fond of neglected genre pictures, and unusually attentive to how editing and score change the moral temperature of a scene.

The voice of the site reflects that sensibility: measured, conversational, occasionally severe, but never interested in the easy dunk. Mara’s reviews are meant to feel like the thoughtful argument you keep replaying after a screening, when the images are still settling and the stronger details begin to separate themselves from the noise.
